OUR VISION

To yearly have a minimum of hundred (100) teenagers, completely built in the whole doctrine of Christ, living a balanced life between Spirituality, Academia, and Morals.

HOW WE STARTED

Esther Oluwadamilola Agboola

Esther Oluwadamilola Agboola is a passionate lover of God, with an earnest desire to see teenagers thrive against all odds, not just in their academics but also in their spiritual life and morals. She received the vision for teenage ministry in 2021, and decided to engage few teenagers from church in one-on-one conversations, checking on them during the week via telephone calls, praying with them occasionally, alongside other hospitality treats. In 2022, she received the vision concerning those that will embark on the journey towards the teenage ministry, and together in 2023, ‘The Balanced Teenagers’ group was founded.

Esther graduated from secondary school in 2013 as the overall best graduating student (Gladys High School) and proceeded to complete her first degree in Accounting at Joseph Ayo Babalola University, where she bagged a First-Class Honors. On her convocation day, she was the sole recipient of a scholarship from University College of Social Science (UCSS), Poland, to complete her Master’s degree. For reasons best known to her, she forfeited the scholarship and proceeded to complete her professional qualification. In 2018, she completed her professional examination and became inducted in 2019 as an associate member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). That same year (2019), she attended a French school for approximately 6 months, in a bid to embrace diversity of language. Also, in same year, she was opportune to receive another scholarship to complete her Masters in the United Kingdom, and this time around, she embraced the scholarship and proceeded to complete her second degree in Accounting and Finance at University of Nottingham, United Kingdom, United Kingdom where she graduated with Distinction in 2020.

Upon arrival in the UK in 2019, she applied for CPA Australia Strategic Management Accounting Scholarship. And out of all the universities in UK, the scholarship was awarded to just 2 students; she was one out of the two awardees. She is currently on the final stage of completing a professional qualification to become an associate member of Association of Certified Chartered Accountants (ACCA), and Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Australia. Esther is a professional baker and has recently commenced her Doctorate Degree.

Modupeoluwa Grace Akande (nee Opoola)

Modupeoluwa Grace Akande (nee Opoola), is a certified registered dietitian by Institute of Dietetics in Nigeria (IDN). She is a graduate of Nutrition and Dietetics from Obafemi Awolowo University with over eight years experience in leadership training. Her ultimate goal in life has always be to teach and train the younger generations on how to develop the potentials within them so that they’ll use it to solve the problems in their immediate environment.

Her mantra is “be the change you want to see around you”; this spurs her interest in researching more on how she can solve the clothing needs of an average Nigeria through revamping their used clothes into new one using tie-dye (adire), and her continuous progress in the fashion sector made her one of the beneficiary of Tony Elumelu Business Grant in 2019.

In 2020, she leverage more on social media in order to reach her target audiences which are the teenagers by making a daily post tagged “The Gist” on Facebook. Her ability to write has impacted hundreds of people on Facebook which has also made her a winner of Youthlead turning point essay contest where she came 2nd out of all the 160 entries from around the world.

Her resilient nature made her to also be one of alumni of Cheri Blair Foundation for women entrepreneur, alumni of Enterprise Development Center (EDC) for female entrepreneur and currently became an alumnus of FATE foundation in partnership with Google.

She has partnered with youths and professionals to empowered over 1000 women and young adult across five states in Nigeria through her yearly empowerment program under the flagship of PetalsHub Creations; a fashion business she is currently still operating.

She is a wife, mother and mentor to numerous teenagers. She is also one of the team members of The Balanced Teenagers (TBT), a platform set up to raise a total teen and guides them in their spiritual, physical and academic pursuit.

Anuoluwapo Mary Omilani (nee Oladosu)

Anuoluwapo Mary Omilani (nee Oladosu) is an inspiring and energetic woman, a young statistician with up to date knowledge in Data analysis and interpretation, she is driven with love and passion for the kingdom work and passionate in the development of youth and teens in this generation and she believes in building a strong foundation for the young ones. She loves to teach young ones and see them walk in their purpose. She hails from Gbongan in Osun State, Nigeria. She was however born and raised in Ibadan, Oyo State.

She obtained her primary certificate from Randolph schools and secondary school certificate from Unity school Ejigbo, Osun State emerging as the best student in Mathematics and Further Mathematics out of over 200 students. She got her Bsc degree in Statistics from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology in the year 2021,she is also certified in Project Management and other professional courses. With her quest to know more about her profession, she is currently doing her diploma in Data analytics at SQI college of ICT. She obtained her national youth service corps certificate in 2022 in Ogun State.

She is a member of the national association for Statisticians (NAS), She leads Africa (SLA), Women for Digitals(WG) and Statisticians in the Pharmaceutical industries (PSI). She was awarded the most dependable (female) during her service in school.

Just because of her love for young ones she always celebrate her birthday with teens and young children especially the less privileged every year. This made her to accept the offer to be part of the team leaders of The Balanced Teenagers(TBT), a group to nurture and help young ones in every aspect of life.

She is married to Omilani Ifeoluwa Moses, a lover of God and a Pharmacist by profession.

Mary Titilayo Akinbisoye

Mary Titilayo Akinbisoye, the third of four children, is a single woman hailing from Ondo State, specifically Ondo town. Her life radiates with a profound love for God, fueled by an earnest mission to spread the gospel of Jesus, beckoning souls to walk with God.

In moments of leisure, Mary loves to write songs of worship and creates new dress style. Her guiding principle, "This life is too short; let's do well," encapsulates her philosophy on living with intention and purpose.

Mary's educational journey began at Future Leaders for her primary education, followed by Hebron College for her secondary schooling. Subsequently, she pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Mass Communication from the esteemed Joseph Ayo Babalola University (JABU), achieving the distinction of graduating with honors, specifically a Second Class Upper division.

Immersed in the realm of communication, Mary serves as a dedicated Customer Service Representative, currently holding the position of Team Lead at Acorns Africa Investment, a reputable financial institution. Her unwavering dedication to professional growth is evident through her pursuit of various certifications, with a hunger for more knowledge propelling her forward.

Beyond her professional pursuits, Mary's compassionate spirit led to the establishment of The Resonance Foundation (TRF), a non-profit organization she founded and leads as its visionary president. TRF is committed to supporting the physically challenged and underprivileged members of society, having successfully conducted four impactful outreach programs to date.

Mary Titilayo Akinbisoye epitomizes compassion, determination, and a relentless drive to make a positive difference in the lives of others. Through her multifaceted endeavors, she continues to inspire and uplift those around her, leaving an indelible mark of kindness and generosity in her wake.
